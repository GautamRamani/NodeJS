Cookies: Cookies are small pieces of data that a web browser stores on the user's device. 
         They can be set by the server and sent back and forth with each request and response. 
         Cookies are typically stored on the client-side (user's device).

Sessions: Sessions, on the other hand, involve server-side storage. 
          A session typically starts when a user logs in and ends when they log out or when their session expires. 
          Session data is stored on the server.
